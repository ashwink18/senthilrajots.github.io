Collective

The OTS Collective is a set of ORIGINALS by various artists/bands from
“On The Streets of Chennai”
Community Each collective offers a bouquet of songs,
each with a unique signature and emotion

Our Vision:
1. A new edition of collective will be out every 3 months.
2. We hope to expand our collective offering across languages.
3. We are open to collaborating with communities/experts in other Art forms (Dance, sand art, theatre, etc).
4. On The Streets of Chennai to be a hub for Indie musicians to come together and thrive.

What started as a small group of five to seven people in January 2019, now houses almost 150 members and 2000 followers. Members of On The Streets have been performing originals, covers and remixes, spanning genres, in public spaces since its inception and spreading smiles along the way. Some of the prestigious venues include Railway Stations, Metro stations, Beaches, Parks, Pondy Bazaar and various malls dotted across the city.
